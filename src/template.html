About me

Early on, my lifelong dream was to work with computers, hardwares, softwares, it really didn't matter. I have a thirst for knowledge, and look for opportunities to apply that knowledge in an ever-changing world. This is not exclusive to the IT field, but extends outward to the very fabric of society, including business management, work environments and labor relations, social, economic, political, religious issues, and more. I welcome open dialog from all walks of life, and have an appreciation for diversity, traditionalism, and robust but reasonable debate. I am an active participant in life, rather than allowing myself to be swept along with the current.
